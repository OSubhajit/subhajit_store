The Author

About Me

I am Subhajit Sarkar — a writer and developer from Assam, India. I write stories that come from real emotions, quiet moments, and the things people carry inside them without saying aloud.

My writing explores themes of love, freedom, loss, and the small moments that change everything. Every book I write is meant to stay with the reader — long after the last page.

This store is where my creative work lives. Thank you for being here.

— Subhajit Sarkar
